JONATHAN RAINEY

My name is Jonathan Rainey. I am a graphic designer who is trying to master the skills of graphics and designs. My goal as a graphic designer is to hone the skills of being a creative person and become one of the greatest artist. I had a very artistic upbringing and started drawing with pencils at the age of 5. Ever since then I’ve became proficient in artistic materials such as markers, crayons, chalk, etc. It wasn’t until I got in high school that I knew what being a graphic designer was.

Since then I took an interest in learning how to create art digitally. Since graduating high school I’ve started a online business on the side where I would sell merchandise with my graphics on them and also thrifted pieces I find. All of this happened while in college. I push myself beyond my limits because I know I can become successful in anything I create and only want perfection. Thank you for reading this far and I hope you enjoy the rest of my portfolio and the work that I have done thus far.
